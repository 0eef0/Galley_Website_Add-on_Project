The Art of the Household Lamp

What is a lamp?

Well, lamps are the light of every home's life.

However, they can be more than just that. For most people, lamps are a thing to look up to; a thing to appreciate. Here, we will showcase the admirability of various lamps everywhere so you can appreciate them just a little more.

The History of Lamps

Picture this, it is October 1979. Everyone is using candles and lanturns to light their houses. Then all of a sudden, our lord and savior Mr. Thomas Edison created the lamp, after countless experiments. It is because of this gift that we are able to live our daily lives.
